📜 HOW TO PLAY BLACKJACK ROYALE

🎯 Goal: Beat the dealer's hand without exceeding 21.

🃏 Card Values: Number cards = face value. Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10. Ace = 11 or 1.

⚙️ Gameplay Rules:

  • Place bet (min 25 points) → Click "PLACE BET" then "NEW HAND".
  • HIT → Take another card. STAND → End your turn.
  • DOUBLE → Double bet, receive exactly one more card.
  • SPLIT → If first two cards have same rank, split into two hands.
  • Insurance/Even Money offered when dealer shows Ace.
  • Blackjack pays 3:2. Dealer stands on soft 17.
  • Complete 50 hands maximum.
